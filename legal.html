Talent Agreement (Plain-Language)

Applies to: Talent / Members Last updated: 2026-02-17

This defines membership expectations, branding rights, content ownership, conduct, and what happens if someone leaves.

0) No guarantee

Submitting an application does not guarantee acceptance. Membership can be declined at our discretion.

1) Ownership

Default: You own your character, model, and content unless a separate written agreement says otherwise.

2) Kuroveil branding & representation

  • You may use Kuroveil branding while you are an active member in good standing.
  • Kuroveil name/logo/brand stay with Kuroveil.
  • Do not represent yourself as speaking “for Kuroveil” unless approved staff.
  • On departure, remove Kuroveil branding from profiles/panels within a reasonable timeframe.

3) Minimum activity

  • Minimum: 2 streams or uploads per week (unless approved hiatus)
  • Communicate clearly and stay active in Discord
  • Show up for scheduled collabs/events when possible (with notice if you can’t)

4) Hiatus / inactivity

If you need a break, request a hiatus. Extended inactivity without communication may result in removal.

5) Conduct

  • No harassment, hate, doxxing, threats, or targeted drama.
  • No “bait applications”, ghosting during onboarding, or bad-faith participation.
  • Follow platform ToS (Twitch/YouTube/etc.). Repeated violations can be grounds for removal.

6) Collab content & promo permission

  • Collab VODs/clips remain on the uploader’s channel.
  • Members allow reasonable reposting of clips (with credit) for Kuroveil promotion.
  • Kuroveil does not claim ownership of your individual content.

7) Leaving / removal

Members may leave at any time (ideally with notice). Kuroveil may remove members for inactivity, conduct, or breaking rules. Removal includes roster delist + role removal.

8) Suspension

In serious cases (harassment, doxxing, repeated ToS violations), Kuroveil may suspend access immediately while staff reviews the situation.

Important: If you commissioned art/assets through Kuroveil funds (rare), ownership/usage may be handled by a separate written agreement.

Community Guidelines

Applies to: Discord / Community Last updated: 2026-02-17

These rules keep the server clean, safe, and focused on growth.

Core rules

  • Respect others. No harassment, slurs, or targeted attacks.
  • No doxxing, threats, or calls for harassment.
  • No spam, raids, or disruptive behavior.
  • No NSFW where it doesn’t belong (follow channel rules).

Collabs & boundaries

  • Get consent before clipping private calls or posting behind-the-scenes content.
  • Respect “no topics” boundaries set by members.
  • Do not pressure others into collabs, voice calls, or off-platform contact.

Moderation

Moderation actions may include warnings, timeouts, and bans. Appeals go through staff.

Safety & privacy

  • Do not share personal info (yours or others).
  • Report suspicious DMs/scams to staff.
