Founder’s Promise

Clarity up front. No hidden control grabs — just structure, collabs, and growth systems.

What you keep
  • Your identity + channels. Your brand stays yours. You’re not “owned.”
  • Your model assets. You keep what you make/commission (unless you choose otherwise and rules are subject to change).
  • Your freedom. You can leave — with simple, written rules (see Legal).
What Kuroveil provides
  • Collab scheduling + content planning cadence
  • Clip pipeline expectations + cross-promo structure
  • Brand coherence + template systems (thumbnails, captions, etc.)
Quick rules (simple)
Keep it clean, consistent, and team-safe.
  • Be active + communicate if you can’t be.
  • Respect others’ boundaries + scheduling.
  • No drama farming using the group name.
  • Leaving is allowed — we just coordinate the transition.

FAQ

Fast answers to the questions people actually ask before joining a group.

Do I keep my identity, channels, and branding?
Yes. Your channels and identity stay yours. Kuroveil is structure + collabs + systems — not ownership. Any exceptions would be opt-in and written.
Are there fees to join?
No required fees. If we ever do optional group assets (like shared graphics or collab overlays), we’ll be transparent and you can opt out.
What’s the minimum schedule expectation?
“Consistent” beats “perfect.” We look for steady streaming + a clip habit. Exact requirements are on the Requirements page.
Can I be in other groups?
Usually yes — as long as it doesn’t conflict with our branding/scheduling expectations. The rule is: don’t create confusion, and don’t sabotage collabs.
How do collabs work in Kuroveil?
We plan in short cycles: a collab calendar + assigned “clip moments” + cross-post expectations. Simple systems that actually get executed.
What happens if I want to leave?
You can leave. We coordinate a clean transition (branding + announcements) and follow the written terms. See Legal.
Still unsure?
Ask in Discord — we’d rather answer questions than get the wrong applicants.
