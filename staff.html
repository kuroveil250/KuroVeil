How It Works

Apply, get reviewed, then we move fast. This is designed to stay simple and scalable.

1) ApplyPick a role and submit your best examples.
2) ReviewRolling review (we check continuously).
3) ShortlistIf shortlisted, we message you (Discord/email).
4) Trial TaskSmall paid test to confirm fit + quality.
5) RotationYou’re added to our ongoing staff rotation.
Response expectations: If you’re shortlisted, expect a message within a few days. If you don’t hear back, we may be full for that role or still reviewing.

FAQ

Answers to the questions applicants actually ask.

Is this paid?

Most work is paid per deliverable or per scope. Social Support pay depends on workload. If a role is unpaid/limited, we’ll state it upfront.

Do you use contracts?

Yes, for paid work. We use simple written agreements that clarify deliverables, payment, and usage.

How many revisions do you allow?

Depends on the deliverable. Usually 1–2 revision rounds for most projects unless otherwise agreed.

How do you deliver files?

Usually via Google Drive/Dropbox links. We’ll specify formats (MP4, PNG, PSD, etc.) in the brief.

Can I apply to multiple roles?

Yes — but choose your primary strength. If you apply to more than one, keep it clean and include only your best work for each.

How long does review take?

Rolling review. If shortlisted, you’ll usually hear back within a few days. If you don’t, we may be full or still reviewing.
